Meet The Doctors

of Dental Professionals on Whitesburg

Sonya L. Wintzell, DMD

General & Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Wintzell’s commitment to her patients is evident through her personal approach to dentistry as well as the ongoing continuing education she receives. She enjoys helping her patients gain renewed confidence through a radiant, healthy smile.

Dr. Wintzell graduated with from Judson College and holds her Doctorate of Medical Dentistry from the University of Alabama School of Dentistry. Dr. Wintzell also has attended the Seattle Institute for Advanced Dental Education, Peter Dawson Seminars, and Carl Misch Implant Institute. In addition, Dr. Wintzell is trained and certified to administer Facial Esthetic Botox and Dermal Fillers. Dr. Wintzell has also received advanced training at the L.D. Pankey Institute and enjoys creating long lasting, beautiful smiles for all of her patients.

When not in the office, Dr. Wintzell enjoys spending time with her husband and twfour children. She enjoys CrossFit, gardening, and reading in her free time.

Dr. Wintzell is a member of the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, the Academy of Implantology, the International Congress of Oral Implantology, the Dental Organization for Conscious Sedation. She is an alumni of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, and is a board member for Judson College and For Life Ministry.

Lisa D. Bruce, DMD, LLC

General & Cosmetic Dentist

Dr. Bruce is dedicated to learning about the newest technologies and techniques in dentistry, ensuring the health and well-being of her patients. She is passionate about her patients and strives to make every dental visit relaxing and tailored to each patient’s unique dental needs. Come visit us today!

Dr. Bruce graduated from Judson College and holds a Doctorate of Medical Dentistry from the University of Alabama School of Dentistry. Dr. Bruce has attended the world-renowned L.D. Pankey Institute where she has served as a teaching assistant. In addition, Dr. Bruce has attended the Seattle Institute for Advanced Dental Education, Peter Dawson Seminars, and Carl Misch Implant Institute. Dr. Bruce is trained and certified to administer Facial Esthetic Botox and Dermal Filler procedures.

When not in the office, Dr. Bruce enjoys spending time with her husband and two children. Her interests include golf and boating.

Dr. Bruce is a member of the American Dental Association, the International Congres of Oral Implantologists, the Academy of General Dentistry, the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, the Academy of Implantology, the American Academy of Facial Esthetics, and is an alumni of the Dental Organization for Conscious Sedation.
